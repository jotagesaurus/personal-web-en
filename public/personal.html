My hobbies

Videdogames

My biggest hobby is video games. I am a passionate player of games with great stories, like The Last of Us or The Legend of Zelda. But I also enjoy casual games where fun comes first, like Super Mario. I never turn down an invitation to play Mario Kart.

Literature

Since I was a child I fell in love with reading and since then books have never left me. I love horror or science fiction literature, but I enjoy any genre as long as its characters and plot captivate me. I also gravitate towards other types of reading such as scientific literature, graphic novels or historical novels. My favorite authors are Stephen King, Haruki Murakami and Carl Sagan.

Movies

Another way to enjoy great stories. I like all types of movies, but my literary tastes tend to coincide with those of the seventh art (Horror and science fiction continue to be my main inclination). I also enjoy a good drama that makes me think about the complex aspects of the human condition or a thriller that makes me think and keeps me on the edge of my seat.

Crafting

When I'm not enjoying a good story, I like to create things with my own hands. Origami is my main hobby in this sense, but I can also direct my creativity to drawing, painting or even modeling clay. I can spend hours assembling Legos or painting a board game miniature (another of my hobbies).

Let's talk!

I am always willing to learn, teach and build together. We are one click away.
