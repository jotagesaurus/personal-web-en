Some projects I have worked on

Remote health service

I was responsible for designing, prototyping, testing, and implementing a telemedicine service through loT devices for remote health care in Colombia. I worked hand in hand with developers, operational staff, physicians, and end users for the design and integration of all service components.

Achievements

  • Multiple platforms integration for use of physicians and patiens
  • Geotracking system implementation for real time monitoring
  • Design of service model, support servicing and pricing strategy
  • Multiple cities implementation with over 3.000 patients attended monthly

Learnings

  • Updating location and contact information is essential for a home service
  • Health care services require special care with compliance with bio-sanitary regulations
  • The training of medical personnel in the use of platforms and devices must be thorough

Service Desk Digital Transformation

I was part of the Service Desk BPO process evolution project as lead researcher and innovation facilitator. For this project I investigated new trends, technologies and tools, with which we built and executed the evolution roadmap.

Achievements

  • Omnichannel service implementation
  • Prototyping and testing of virtual assistant and self-management tools with AI
  • Development of prototypes for Workforce Management web tools to optimize the capacity of service desks.

Learnings

  • The integration of different tools and information systems should be the first step.
  • RPA automation is no longer an evolution component and becomes an improvement enabler or stepping stone to achieve other evolutions

AI Vision for Shopping Malls

I designed and prototyped a monitoring service through artificial vision for shopping centers and large stores. Based on the market needs, I proposed use cases for developers to prototype and develop video processing algorithms with AI.

Achievements

  • Model designed to work with the client's installed infrastructure
  • Scalable architecture, with use cases that can be implemented in a modular way
  • Security and business insights gained with a unified solution.

Learnings

  • Specific network quality and availability requirements were not met by all potential customers
  • Pricing model and policies are key to fit the needs of the targeted market
